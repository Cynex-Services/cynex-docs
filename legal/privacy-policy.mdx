## Privacy Policy

**Thank you for using Cynex!** This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect your data. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy, please reach out to us.

## Information We Collect

1. **Discord User Data**: 
   - We may collect and store certain information provided by Discord users, such as:
     - User IDs
     - Usernames
     - Discriminator numbers
     - Profile pictures  
   This data is used to enhance the user experience within the Discord platform.

2. **Usage Information**: 
   - We gather data on how you interact with Cynex, including:
     - Commands entered
     - Server interactions
     - Other relevant usage data  
   This helps us improve the functionality and performance of Cynex.

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Bot Functionality**: 
   - The information we collect is utilized to provide, maintain, and enhance Cynex's functionality. This includes:
     - Responding to user commands
     - Managing server interactions
     - Delivering a seamless user experience

2. **Analytics**: 
   - We may use aggregated and anonymized data for analytical purposes to understand user behavior, preferences, and trends. This information helps us make informed decisions about updates and improvements.

3. **Security Measures**: 
   - We implement reasonable security measures to protect the collected information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure.

## Data Sharing

- **Updates**: 
  - We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes by posting the revised Privacy Policy on Cynex or other appropriate channels. Your continued use of Cynex following any modifications indicates your acknowledgment of the changes and your consent to abide by the updated policy.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us. 

By using Cynex, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. We appreciate your trust in us with your information.
